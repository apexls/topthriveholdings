Welcome to Top Thrive

Top thrive holdings is a registered holdings company based in Lesotho that owns shares or the controlling stocks in other companies. We own shares in companies like Top Transport, Top Wool and Mohair, Top Construction and many other businesses. The above mentioned companies are all based in Lesotho but they also operate beyond borders. We also fund and invest in small scale start up business and help them grow by giving them access to our resources ande equipment.

Management

Monethi Ramakalima

CEO & FOUNDER

Mr. Monethi Ramakalima is a prominent mosotho businessman. He has multiple businesses registerd under his name. He started his first business in 2000, a small grocery shop.In 2004 he opened a boutique. With the profits from his businesses, he ventured into the wool and mohair industry. This was where he got his big break through. In 2008 he built his first shearing shed( Top wool and mohair), that buys precious fibres from basotho farmers and sells them to international clients. Due to the need of transporting the fibres, he started a logistics company(Top transport) that transports fibres an other products to the global market. With abunbdant resources at his hands, he went into construction, where he started his construction company(Top construction).

Trading

Top Wool & Mohair

Top Wool & Mohair is a leading wool & mohair broker, licensed to market, auction, prepare and export Lesotho's fibres(wool and mohair) to international markets. We market Lesotho's clip to worldwide markets and currently have good relationships with top makers in Asia, Europe and South america to whom we sell our fibres to. Through our affiliation with traders and our long lasting trust with farmers, our annual sales capacity is over 1,800 tons of wool and over 400 tons of mohair. We work with Lesotho's traders and farmers to improve quality of our fibres through constant engagements with stakeholders to understand the shift in markets and how we can promote Lesotho's wool and mohair to other markets.

Top Transport

Top Transport is a mosotho owned logistics company based in maputsoe. We specialize in the movement or transportation of all types of cargo from and to Lesotho. With our well maintained and road worthy trucks, we are guaranteed to deliver your cargo to its final destination within the shortest time possible and still making sure that it is as the same as when you handed it over to us. Construction

Top Construction

Top construction is a mosotho owned registered construction company based in Maputsoe, Lesotho. We handle everything that has to do with construction. We are also a mass supplier of construction material(Sand, bricks, rough sand, cement, concrete and many more..) and equipment & tools(Spades, concrete machines, helmets, excavators, bulldozers and many more..).
